    How to read this

    The bars compare each sector to the Nasdaq-100 over the past month. Bar to the right = beating the market (money flowing in). Bar to the left = trailing it (money flowing out). The line under each bar is this week’s turn — a sector deep in the red but green this week may be waking up.

    The little dots show how many of each sector’s stocks are still in an uptrend (above their 50-day average price). Mostly filled = healthy. Mostly hollow = damaged.

    Tap any sector to see its stocks. “Setup score” (0–100) rates how close a stock is to a buyable recovery — 70+ is interesting.

    This page refreshes itself every 15 minutes during US market hours. Same transparent math as the full research dashboard.
